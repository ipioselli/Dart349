HOW TO SPOT FAST FASHION BRANDS

Sorry no sale here

*We are trying to bring awareness on fast fashion and over consumption*

  • They have thousands of styles which follow all the latest trends
  • They have an extremely short turnaround from when the clothing item is seen on the catwalk/celebrity.
  • Manufacturing is done in low-income countries in which workers are paid very low wages.
  • Materials are very low quality such as polyester which cause consumers to discard after only a few uses.
  • The websites have limited quantities to get consumers to buy as fast as possible before the items sell out.
