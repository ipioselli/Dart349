ABOUT THE BRAND

Style City is the world's leading fast fashion company. We are renowed for making the cheapest quality clothing in the shortest amount of time!

Our mission as a fast fashion brand is to give you the cheapest and trendiest clothing in the shortest amount of time so that they do not last long. This keeps you buying more and feeds into the never ending consumerist cycle.

We want you to keep throwing away your old clothes and buying new ones so that we can continue to exploit our workers and ruin our planet!
